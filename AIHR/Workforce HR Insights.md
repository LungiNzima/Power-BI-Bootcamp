The dashboard integrates two primary data sets: one capturing employee demographics and work-related attributes such as gender, tenure, and business travel, and another focused on job satisfaction metrics. Using Power BI, these datasets were transformed and analyzed to generate meaningful insights. The resulting dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce trends and employee engagement.

📑 **HR Insights** 

_Workforce Demographics:_ The workforce consists of 1470 employees. 60% of which are female, and the majority are between the ages of 30-35 years. Diversity is healthy but leadership roles should be edited for gender balance. Succession planning is needed to prepare younger employees for senior roles. 

_Performance & Satisfaction:_ Average Performance Rating is a moderate score of 3.15/5 and Job Satisfaction is a below average score of 2.73/5. Low engagement is a critical retention risk. Performance development programs should be aligned with engagement intiatives. 

_Attrition & Tenure:_ Key attrition drivers are: Overtime (2.93x higher attrition risk), Tenure (≤ 1 year → 2.70x higher attrition risk), Frequent Business Travel (1.77x higher attrition risk.) and Low Job Satisfaction (1.58x higher attrition risk). Improving workload management and onboarding processes are urgent priorities. To reduce turnover travel fatigue and dissatisfaction must be addressed. 

🔑 **Recommendations**

_Boost Job Satisfaction:_ Conduct pulse surveys to identify dissatisfaction drivers. Enhance recognition, career development, and manager training.

_Manage Workload & Overtime:_ Monitor overtime trends and redistribute tasks. Introduce flexible work arrangements and wellness programs.

_Strengthen Early Retention:_ Improve onboarding and mentorship for employees in their first year. Provide clear career pathways to reduce early exits.

_Reduce Travel Fatigue:_ Encourage virtual alternatives to frequent travel. Offer rest days or travel perks to support employees.

_Performance Development:_ Invest in training, coaching, and performance management systems. Align performance goals with employee engagement initiatives.

_Succession Planning:_ Build leadership pipelines for mid-career employees. Ensure gender diversity in leadership roles.
